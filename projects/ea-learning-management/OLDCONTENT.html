EA Learning Management

Creating a comprehensive learning platform that empowers EA employees to develop their skills and advance their careers.

Learning Experience
UX Design
Employee Development

The Challenge

EA needed a modern learning management system that could:

  • Support diverse learning needs across global teams
  • Provide personalized learning paths
  • Track and measure learning progress
  • Enable social learning and knowledge sharing
  • Integrate with existing HR systems

Strategic Approach

Discovery

  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Learning needs assessment
  • Technical requirements gathering
  • Competitive analysis

Implementation

  • Platform architecture design
  • Content strategy development
  • User interface design
  • Integration planning

Key Features

  • Personalized learning paths based on role and goals
  • Interactive course content with multimedia support
  • Progress tracking and certification management
  • Social learning features and peer reviews
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Analytics dashboard for learning metrics

Impact & Results

  • 95% employee activation rate
  • 70% increase in course completion rates
  • 40% reduction in training costs
  • 89% user satisfaction score
  • 3x increase in mobile learning engagement

Project Details

  • Role: Lead UX Designer
  • Duration: 12 months
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe XD
  • Team: 4 designers, 6 engineers
