Salesforce CRM

Brand Customization Interface for Web and Mobile - Enabling companies to design and launch branded mobile apps for their employees.

UX Design
Mobile App
Brand Customization

The Challenge

Salesforce, as a leading B2B CRM (Customer Relationship Management) Software, needed a solution to:

  • Enable companies to design and launch branded mobile apps for their employees
  • Provide a simple way to customize the Salesforce mobile app
  • Increase user adoption through branded experiences
  • Create a marketing tool for stakeholder buy-in

Key Use Cases

Employee Adoption

Companies needed to brand custom versions of the Salesforce app and publish it on app stores to quickly socialize it within their organization and increase usage adoption.

Stakeholder Buy-in

The solution needed to serve as a marketing tool to demonstrate value and get buy-in from key stakeholders within companies.

Solution

We developed a comprehensive brand customization interface that allows companies to:

  • Customize app appearance with company branding
  • Preview changes in real-time
  • Generate branded app builds for app store submission
  • Manage multiple brand variations

Project Details

  • Role: Interface Designer
  • Duration: 6 months
  • Tools: Sketch, InVision, Photoshop
  • Team: Designer, Developer, Project Manager

Key Features

  • • Brand customization interface
  • • Real-time preview
  • • App store deployment
  • • Multi-brand management
  • • Analytics dashboard
