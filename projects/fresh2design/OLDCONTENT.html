Fresh2Design Rebrand

Transforming Austin's leading design community through strategic rebranding and platform redesign.

Brand Design
Community Platform
Event Design

The Challenge

Fresh2Design needed to evolve its brand and platform to better serve its growing community of over 3,500 members. Key challenges included:

  • Modernizing the visual identity while maintaining community recognition
  • Creating a more engaging digital platform for event management
  • Developing a scalable system for various event types and formats
  • Building stronger connections within the design community

Strategic Approach

Community Research

  • Member surveys and interviews
  • Event attendance analysis
  • Community engagement metrics
  • Platform usage patterns

Brand Development

  • Visual identity refresh
  • Platform redesign
  • Event branding system
  • Community guidelines

Key Initiatives

The rebranding project encompassed several major initiatives:

  • New visual identity system with flexible applications
  • Redesigned community platform with improved event features
  • Event series branding (including SXSW Interactive events)
  • Community engagement programs and storytelling events
  • Partnerships with leading tech companies and design studios

Notable Events

  • SXSW: Adobe XD on "Design Evolution"
  • LGBTQ Voices in Tech storytelling event
  • Hurricane Harvey Relief Pin Show fundraiser
  • Monthly software education panels
  • Design leadership workshops

Impact & Results

  • Grew community from 2,000 to 3,500+ members
  • 40% increase in event attendance
  • Successful partnerships with Adobe, IBM, and other tech leaders
  • Created sustainable model for community growth
  • Established Fresh2Design as Austin's premier design community

Project Details

  • Role: Branding & Operations Director
  • Duration: 2015-2019
  • Tools: Adobe CC, Sketch, Webflow
  • Team: Design, Events, Community

Key Achievements

  • • Community growth to 3,500+

  • • Corporate partnerships
  • • Fundraising initiatives
  • • Educational programs
