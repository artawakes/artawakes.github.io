Forkright Brand System

Creating a cohesive and scalable brand system that reflects Forkright's mission to revolutionize the food service industry.

Brand Design
Visual Identity
Design System

The Challenge

Forkright needed a comprehensive brand system that would:

  • Establish a distinctive visual identity in the food service industry
  • Create consistency across all digital and physical touchpoints
  • Support rapid scaling and product development
  • Reflect their innovative approach to food service solutions

Strategic Approach

Discovery Phase

  • Brand audit and analysis
  • Stakeholder interviews
  • Market positioning research
  • Competitor analysis

Development Phase

  • Visual identity creation
  • Design system architecture
  • Component library development
  • Implementation guidelines

Brand Elements

The brand system encompasses several key components:

  • Logo system with flexible applications
  • Color palette inspired by fresh ingredients
  • Typography system for digital and print
  • Icon library and illustrations
  • Photography style guide
  • Voice and tone guidelines

Implementation

The brand system was implemented across multiple touchpoints:

  • Digital products and platforms
  • Marketing materials and collateral
  • Social media presence
  • Environmental graphics
  • Internal communications

Project Details

  • Role: Lead Brand Designer
  • Duration: 4 months
  • Tools: Figma, Adobe CC, Principle
  • Team: Design, Marketing, Development
