Why we stay tied to one brand

These aren't internal tools nobody sees. They're the brand itself, in front of the retailers and advertisers we're trying to win and keep. Quality perception is already one of the top complaints partners raise, and it directly risks adoption of our self-serve tools. If every team drifts and builds its own look, we fragment that experience one inconsistent screen at a time. TDS is what keeps every partner-facing surface unmistakably ours, and makes the whole suite faster to build.
