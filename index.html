Pixels, Person, and Purpose

I started in Buenos Aires with architecture and technical drawing, spent 15 years in graphic design and marketing in LA, then moved into product design. For nine years I've designed enterprise products at EA, Lyft, and Instacart, from a global intranet to a unified workspace to order and fulfillment tools. At Instacart, building the design system became the whole job: I founded the Tooling Design System and scaled it to 144+ components, now used 786K+ times in 18 teams at 90.9% satisfaction.

What keeps me going is making complicated things feel simple and human, whether that's helping a shopper find their groceries faster or helping a team build tools with less friction.

AI & Design Engineering

Closing the gap between design and code

Lately my work lives in the space between design and engineering. Design systems usually exist as two copies, one in Figma and one in code, that drift apart over time. Closing that gap is what I care about most right now: token systems that match the code one to one, with AI doing the slow parts like migration and catching drift, while still leaving room for creative exploration. The future is design and engineering working from one source of truth.
